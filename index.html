Soothe Your Nerves

Sometimes it feels like everything in modern life is about doing more. Maximizing productivity, buying, selling, pushing, getting, making it happen.

What if we gave the same amount of attention to the opposite?

Just "be"-ing. Chilling out. Relaxing. Taking a break. Letting it all go for awhile.

What happens when we care about the quality of our breaks and downtime just as much as the to-do list?

Sometimes the most profoundly impactful thing is to do nothing at all.

Develop the skill of true rest and learn more about why it is so important.

  • Guided 5 to 10-minute breaks
  • Wise quotations to inspire you to do less
  • Release your worries with the self-help auto-therapist
  • Learn about the human nervous system and how it finds balance, rhythm, and regulation
  • Resources for further learning and practice
